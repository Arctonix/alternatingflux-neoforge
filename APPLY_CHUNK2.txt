CHUNK 2 — AF TRANSFORMER (the HV <-> AF step-down)
===================================================
This is the last FUNCTIONAL piece. It lets power get onto an AF line: feed HV into
one side, AF comes out the other (and vice versa), 1:1 conversion. After this, a
full chain works: HV source -> AF transformer -> AF line -> AF transformer -> HV machine.

HOW IT WORKS
  - AFTransformerBlockEntity extends IE's TransformerBlockEntity, overriding
    getHigherWiretype()="AF" and acceptableLowerWires={HV}. IE's generic canAttach()
    enforces one higher + one lower wire. Rate = min(left,right), automatic.
  - 3-tall multiblock (like IE's HV transformer): place on the ground, it occupies
    the clicked block + 2 above. Uses IE's "basic_split" model loader to render the
    ported transformer OBJ across the multiblock, with a mirrored variant.
